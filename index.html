Fresh Berry Cake

Below is a vanilla cake I made for the 4th of July. It has a whipped cream icing with strawberries and blueberries in the middle and on top for decoration. It is a great choice for summer when you want something light and fresh. I shared it with my co-workers and they really enjoyed it.

Fruity Pebbles Marshmallow Cookies

Below are some cookies I made just for fun. I love fruity pebbles cereal and thought it would be cool to use them in cookies. The icing includes marshmallow fluff. It turned out to be a great combination. This batch didn't make it long enough to share with others!

Pumpkin Pie

Below is a pumpkin pie I made for Thanksgiving one year. The crust is homemade, and I added a leaf on top for some decoration. The family really enjoyed it! I am the designated dessert maker for Thanksgiving and had to make two pies the following year!

Lemon Blueberry Cheesecake

Below is lemon blueberry cheesecake I made as a birthday cake. It has blueberry swirls and a lemon base with a graham cracker crust. Cheesecakes can be tricky to bake, but this one turned out well!
